  In this article, we will discuss using GeoGebra to present and connect
  multiple representations. The purpose of the activities shared is to help
  students reflect on the consequences of different units when graphing
  trigonometric functions. Specifically, the activity should help students
  compare the consequences, with respect to the rate of change, of using
  radians or degrees when graphing trigonometric functions.
