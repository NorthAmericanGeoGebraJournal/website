abstract: |
  In this paper, I describe a classroom experience on how I guided a group of
  preservice secondary mathematics teachers to extend, formulate, and refine a
  conjecture about the ratio of the area of a pentagon and the area of its
  medial pentagon: The ratio of the area of a medial pentagon to the area of
  the outer pentagon lies between 1/2 and 3/4. The use of GeoGebra facilitated
  the discovery and visualization of the conjecture.
