abstract: |
  Students will be tasked with finding the best location for a firehouse given
  houses along a street, which are represented as points on a line. The
  worksheet and accompanying GeoGebra sketch will not tell students how to
  approach the task, leading students to practice reasoning skills. In this
  paper, I will argue that rich tasks such as this one will benefit students
  more on end of course assessments under the Common Core and PARCC, and in
  mathematics classes in general.
