abstract: |
  Using GeoGebra, we present an innovative method for teaching of the
  intersection of polyhedrons with a plane using infinite points and the swap
  of finite and infinite points. The method presented is efficient and
  powerful, allowing one to generate solutions of a whole set of problems by
  solving one instance and using a pre-made applet at any stage of the
  solution process.
