abstract: |
  The equilateral triangle provides a rich context for students and teachers
  to explore and discover geometrical relations using GeoGebra. In this paper,
  we provide teachers with interactive applets to use in their classrooms to
  support student conjecturing regarding properties of the equilateral
  triangle. Proofs of the properties are then presented. Proofs make use of
  theorems in geometry, trigonometry, coordinate geometry, as well as
  inequalities about numbers.
