abstract: |
  In this paper, we use complex-number operations to carry out transformations
  of geometric shapes and establish connections between geometry and algebra
  in the high-school curriculum. We use dynamic geometry software to visualize
  the geometric effect of these algebraic operations and connect
  complex-number operations to translations, rotations, and dilations.
