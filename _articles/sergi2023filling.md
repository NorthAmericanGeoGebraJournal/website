abstract: |
  When discussing and analyzing functional dependencies, schoolbooks, and
  teachers often use different filling curves—asking students to match various
  vessels with their corresponding graphs. After presenting the three basic
  ideas of functional thinking, the authors demonstrate ways of determining
  functional equations from analyses of filling data and discuss the use of
  dynamic applets with students.
