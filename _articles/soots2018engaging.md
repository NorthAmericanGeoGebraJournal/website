abstract: |
  Soots completed an action research study with pre-algebra students during a
  unit on linear functions. The goals of this study were to increase
  student-led technology engagement and utilize a graphing application that
  would allow students to make connections between multiple representations of
  linear functions. The use of GeoGebra combined with a student-led approach
  to instruction positively impacted the classroom environment, allowed
  students to make connections between representations of functions, and
  supported student discovery, especially within the topic of systems of
  equations.
