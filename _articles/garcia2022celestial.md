abstract: |
  The article presents a celestial sphere model made in GeoGebra. The authors
  describe the math- ematical tools necessary to model celestial objects using
  spherical coordinates and the construction of stars, circles, and meridians.
  They incorporate a brief description of the construction of constellations
  using GeoGebra through JavaScript. In addition, the article describes the
  modeling of the ecliptic and the positions of the Sun and the Earth to
  understand their apparent movements and the concept of the Zodiac.
