  This article presents a digital celestial planisphere for the northern
  hemisphere created with GeoGebra. It describes all the necessary elements of
  this tool and how it is built as a projection of the celestial sphere onto a
  plane. It also details its construction and presents examples of educational
  activities that can be implemented in astronomy classrooms and workshops.
