abstract: |
  Abstract: The author has presented 20 workshops teaching high school and
  college teachers to use GeoGebra. The formats have included one, two, and
  four hour workshops at professional meetings, all day in service sessions at
  high schools, and week long on line workshops through the MAA PREP program.
  One indication of the successfulness of the workshops is that a significant
  number of participants in the workshops have subsequently made presentations
  of their own at professional meetings. This paper explores working
  principles that should inform preparation of training material and gives an
  outline of material that is already available and what still remains to be
  developed.
