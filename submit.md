# Submit a Manuscript

The North American GeoGebra Journal welcomes submissions related to
the innovative use of GeoGebra in mathematics education.

## Submission Process

The journal is transitioning to a GitHub-based submission and review
workflow modeled after open peer-review publishing systems.

Authors will submit:

- Manuscript source files
- Abstract
- Author information
- Keywords
- Supporting GeoGebra files
- Figures and supplementary materials

## Manuscript Preparation

Manuscripts should include:

- Title
- Author information
- Abstract
- Keywords
- Main article text
- References
- GeoGebra resources when applicable

## Review Process

Submitted manuscripts undergo peer review by members of the NAGJ
editorial community.

The review process will support:

- Transparent workflows
- Version tracking
- Open collaboration
- Reproducible publication practices


## Submission Portal

The GitHub-based submission system is currently under development.

Please contact the editorial team for submission information.
