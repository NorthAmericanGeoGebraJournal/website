# Editorial Team

The North American GeoGebra Journal is supported by an international
community of mathematics educators, researchers, and GeoGebra users.

## Editors

Editorial team information will be updated during the journal
transition.

## Reviewers

NAGJ relies on a community of reviewers with expertise in:

- Mathematics education
- Technology-enhanced learning
- Dynamic mathematics software
- Teacher preparation
- Educational research


## Join the Reviewer Community

Researchers and educators interested in reviewing for NAGJ are
encouraged to contact the editorial team.
